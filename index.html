COOKING ADOBO

We're about to embark on a flavorful journey, exploring the history, the diverse regional variations,
and most importantly, the step-by-step process of creating the most mouthwatering Adobo you've ever tasted.
So, roll up your sleeves, grab your apron, and let's get ready to elevate your cooking game!

Cooking Method of Adobo

STEP 1 (Preparing The Pot)

Prepare a pot and make it nice and hot over medium heat. Pour some oil and sauté garlic and onions. Add pork and continue sautéing until cooked.

STEP 2 (Prepare the Ingredients)

Prepare the ingredients and Pour Knorr Liquid Seasoning, water, vinegar, laurel leaves, sugar, and pepper. Add 1pc Knorr Pork Cubes. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to a simmer, make sure that you don't cover the pan to evaporate the vinegar. Cook for 10 more minutes.

STEP 3 (Finishing Up)

Remove the meat from the sauce and fry, to add extra crispiness and color to the meat. Return the fried meat in sauce. Reheat and simmer until sauce thickens.
